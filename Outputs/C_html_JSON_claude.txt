## Prompt 4: Decision-Making Information

### Output

## Key Details to Confirm Before Choosing the A-10

**1. What pressure range do you need?**
The measuring range must be selected upfront and defines the maximum working pressure. You also need to know your expected overpressure peaks - if they exceed 1.5-2x the nominal range, the sensor could be damaged.

**2. What type of pressure are you measuring?**
Gauge, absolute, vacuum, or compound - each requires a different configuration, so this needs to be established before ordering.

**3. What accuracy do you actually need?**
Standard accuracy is +/-1% of span. If you need +/-0.25%, you must confirm that the higher-accuracy option is available for your specific configuration - it isn't universal.

**4. What output signal does your system use?**
Your control or monitoring system will expect a specific signal type - 4-20 mA, 0-10 V, 0-5 V, etc. This must match before ordering.

**5. What are your temperature limits?**
Standard operation is 0-80 deg C. If your environment goes outside that, you'll need the extended range (-40 deg C to 100 deg C), which is only available on certain configurations.

**6. What is the process connection on your system?**
Thread type (G 1/4, G 1/2, NPT, etc.) must match your existing pipework or equipment.

**7. What media is being measured?**
The fluid or gas in contact with the sensor must be compatible with stainless steel 316L and your chosen seal material (NBR, FKM, EPDM, etc.). Getting this wrong can cause leaks or sensor failure.

**8. What ingress protection does the environment require?**
IP65 or IP67 availability depends on the electrical connection chosen - confirm the right combination is available if your environment is wet or dusty.

**9. How will it be electrically connected?**
Angular connector, M12, or cable outlet - this affects both installation and ingress protection rating.

**10. Is this a general-purpose application?**
The A-10 is designed for standard industrial use. If your application is highly specialised (extreme pressures, aggressive media, ultra-high precision), a more targeted product may be more appropriate.

---

**In short:** pressure range, media compatibility, output signal, and temperature range are the four most critical factors. Most of the A-10's key specs are configuration-dependent, so these decisions need to be made before placing an order rather than after.
