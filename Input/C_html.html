WIKA A-10 Pressure Transmitter

What this product is

The WIKA A-10 is a pressure transmitter (pressure sensor) designed to measure pressure in industrial systems. It converts pressure into an electrical signal that can be used in monitoring and control systems.

What it is used for

This product is intended for general industrial applications where pressure needs to be measured reliably. Typical use cases include:

  • Machine building
  • Hydraulics and pneumatics systems
  • Pumps and compressors
  • Measurement and control systems

It is designed as a general-purpose solution that can be used across many different types of equipment.

What it measures

The A-10 can be configured to measure different types of pressure:

  • Gauge pressure (relative to atmospheric pressure)
  • Absolute pressure (relative to vacuum)
  • Vacuum and compound pressure

The measuring range depends on the configuration. It can measure very low pressures (starting around 0.05 bar) up to high-pressure applications (up to 1,000 bar or more).

How accurate it is

The accuracy of the device depends on the selected configuration:

  • Typical accuracy is around ±1% of span
  • Higher accuracy options are available with non-linearity down to ±0.25% of span

In addition:

  • Signal noise is typically ≤0.2% of span
  • Long-term drift is low (typically ≤±0.1% of span for higher ranges)
  • Repeatability is stable across operating conditions

This means the product is suitable for applications where consistent and reliable pressure measurement is required.

How it performs in dynamic conditions

For applications where pressure changes quickly, the A-10 can respond rapidly:

  • Settling time can be below 1 ms for certain measuring ranges

This makes it suitable for dynamic systems where fast signal response is important.

How it can be configured

The product is highly configurable, with more than 2 million possible variants. This allows it to be adapted to different technical and installation requirements.

Available output signals include:

  • 4–20 mA (current output)
  • 0–10 V, 0–5 V, 1–5 V (voltage outputs)
  • 0.5–4.5 V (including ratiometric output)

Electrical connection options include:

  • Angular connectors
  • Circular connectors such as M12
  • Cable outlet versions

These options determine how the sensor is connected to the system and how the signal is transmitted.

Process connection and materials

The product connects to the system through different process connections, including:

  • G ¼, G ½
  • NPT threads
  • Other industrial thread standards

Sealing materials can be selected depending on the application:

  • Copper
  • Stainless steel
  • NBR
  • EPDM
  • FKM

Wetted parts are typically made from stainless steel 316L. This makes the product compatible with a wide range of industrial fluids and environments.

Pressure limits and constraints

The maximum working pressure is defined by the selected measuring range and should not be exceeded during normal operation.

The overpressure limit depends on the configuration and is typically between 1.5 and 2 times the nominal measuring range. Operating above these limits can damage the sensor or reduce its accuracy.

Operating conditions

The standard operating temperature range is:

  • 0 to 80°C

Extended configurations allow operation from:

  • -40 to 100°C (depending on signal type and materials)

The device is designed for industrial environments and provides:

  • Resistance to vibration and mechanical shock
  • Ingress protection up to IP65 or IP67 depending on the electrical connection

These characteristics define where and how the product can be safely used.

Installation considerations

The A-10 can be installed in different orientations and configurations. However:

  • Mounting position can introduce small zero point deviations in some cases
  • Different pressure port diameters and optional restrictors are available

Special versions are available for specific requirements:

  • Oil- and grease-free applications
  • Water applications requiring increased stability

These options should be selected based on the operating environment and media.

Reliability and service life

The product is designed for long-term use in industrial environments:

  • Up to 100 million load cycles depending on the measuring range

This reduces the need for maintenance and replacement over time.

Commercial positioning

The A-10 is positioned as a cost-efficient pressure transmitter. It is designed to provide a balance between performance, reliability, and price.

It is available with short delivery times, including custom configurations starting from single-unit orders. This makes it suitable for both OEM production and distribution.

Summary

The WIKA A-10 pressure transmitter is a configurable industrial sensor used to measure pressure and convert it into an electrical signal. It is designed for general-purpose use across many industries, offering a wide measuring range, multiple output options, and reliable performance under typical industrial conditions.
