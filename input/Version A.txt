The WIKA A-10 pressure transmitter is a widely used solution for pressure measurement in general industrial applications, designed to offer reliable performance across a broad range of use cases. This pressure sensor is known as an all-rounder thanks to its flexibility, compact design, and ability to be integrated into many different types of machinery and systems, including hydraulics, pneumatics, pumps, compressors, and general measurement and control environments.

With many years of field-proven performance, the A-10 has been used in countless industrial applications and is appreciated for its consistent quality and long-term reliability. The product is capable of handling up to 100 million load cycles while maintaining stable precision, making it suitable for demanding environments where durability and low maintenance are important considerations. Its robust construction and design allow it to perform well across a variety of conditions, contributing to reduced downtime and replacement costs.

The transmitter supports the measurement of gauge pressure, absolute pressure, and vacuum, depending on configuration, and can be adapted to a wide range of measuring ranges. These can start from very low pressures such as 0.05 bar and extend up to high-pressure applications reaching 1,000 bar or more, depending on the selected configuration. Accuracy varies depending on the measuring range and configuration, with typical values around ±1% of span and options available for improved non-linearity down to ±0.25% of span in certain cases.

The A-10 is highly configurable, with more than 2 million possible variants available, allowing it to be tailored to specific application requirements. Different output signals are supported, including 4–20 mA as well as various voltage outputs such as 0–10 V, 0–5 V, and 1–5 V, along with ratiometric signals. It also supports different electrical connection types, including angular connectors, circular connectors such as M12, and cable outlets, depending on installation needs.

In terms of process connection, the product supports a wide range of thread types such as G ¼, G ½, NPT, and others, with different sealing options including copper, stainless steel, NBR, EPDM, and FKM. The maximum working pressure corresponds to the upper limit of the selected measuring range, and overpressure limits depend on both the measuring range and the selected connection, typically ranging from 1.5 to 2 times the nominal range in many cases.

The device operates across a wide temperature range, typically from 0 to 80°C, with extended options available from -40 up to 100°C depending on configuration and signal type. It is designed to withstand industrial environments, offering resistance to vibration and shock, and supporting ingress protection levels such as IP65 or IP67 depending on the selected electrical connection. The materials used include stainless steel 316L for wetted parts and other industrial-grade materials for external components, ensuring compatibility with a range of media and environments.

From a performance perspective, the transmitter offers low signal noise (typically ≤0.2% of span), good repeatability, and stable long-term behaviour, with drift values typically below ±0.1% of span for higher pressure ranges. It also supports fast response times in many configurations, with settling times below 1 ms for certain measuring ranges, making it suitable for dynamic applications.

The A-10 is also designed with flexibility in mind when it comes to installation and operation, offering different pressure port diameters, optional restrictors, and compatibility with various mounting positions, although certain configurations may introduce small zero point deviations depending on orientation. The product can also be configured for specific media, including oil- and grease-free versions or applications involving water with increased stability requirements.

In addition to its technical capabilities, the A-10 is positioned as a cost-efficient solution, offering a balance between performance and price for a wide range of industrial applications. It is available with short delivery times, even for customised configurations, starting from single-unit orders, which makes it suitable for both OEMs and distributors needing fast turnaround.

Overall, the WIKA A-10 pressure transmitter is a versatile and reliable solution for pressure measurement, combining a wide range of configuration options, proven durability, and solid performance characteristics, making it suitable for many industrial applications where flexibility and cost-efficiency are key factors.
